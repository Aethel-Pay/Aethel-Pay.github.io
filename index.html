Built as an orchestrator, not a custodian

Your money never sits with us

A-Pay doesn't hold funds between a payment and a payout. Every transaction is split at the source — the merchant's share routes directly to their own account, and the platform's fee is taken at the same instant.

That means no float, no settlement lag, and no question of where the money is while it's in transit.
