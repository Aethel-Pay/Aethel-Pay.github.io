How it works

Three steps, no terminal required

Every transaction runs through the same sequence — fast enough that the customer barely notices it happened.

01

Merchant sets the amount

One tap on the dashboard opens a live session for whatever's being charged right now.

02

Customer taps to pay

An NFC tap or a single link takes them straight to a secure checkout — no app to install.

03

Funds settle automatically

Payment confirmation comes back instantly, and the session closes itself out.

Built as an orchestrator, not a custodian

Your money never sits with us

A-Pay doesn't hold funds between a payment and a payout. Every transaction routes directly to your own bank-linked account through Paystack — the same instant the customer pays.

That means no float, no settlement lag, and no question of where the money is while it's in transit.
